About Us

Embark on the voyage of a lifetime with Cruise Everywhere! cruises, where luxury meets adventure on the high seas. Our state-of-the-art cruise ships offer unparalleled comfort and elegance,featuring world-class dining, exhilarating entertainment, and serene wellness retreats. Each journey is meticulously crafted to provide breathtaking excursions and unforgettable experiences at exotic destinations. From sun-soaked Caribbean beaches to the majestic fjords of Alaska, ruise Everywhere! ensures every moment on board is filled with indulgence and discovery. Sail with us and discover a world where your dreams set sail.

  • Fleet Size: 15 state-of-the-art cruise ships
  • Passenger Capacity: Ranges from 1,500 to 5,000 passengers per ship
  • Crew Members: Approximately 1,200 crew members per ship, ensuring a high crew-to-passenger ratio for personalized service
  • Destinations: Over 100 global destinations including the Caribbean, Mediterranean, Northern Europe, Alaska, Australia, and Mexico
  • Dining Options: Over 20 diverse dining venues per ship, including Michelin- starred chef collaborations and themed specialty restaurants
  • Entertainment: Multiple theaters and live performance venues with Broadway-style shows, concerts, and comedy acts
  • Wellness Facilities: Full-service spas, fitness centers, yoga studios, and wellness programs

Cruises

You’re about to discover just what awaits you on a Cruise Everywhere cruise.

Start by exploring the incredible ports we visit and the fun things to do, both on the ship and ashore.

Caribbean

With more than 5,000 islands and cays spread across this amazing region, there’s a lot of paradise to see. The mild climate means it doesn’t matter what time of year you visit. A Caribbean cruise takes you to some of the coolest little hotspots... stretching across the world’s designated hotspot.

The Bahamas

The Bahamas to truly appreciate this classic cruise destination. On this 700- strong string of sun-splashed islands dotting the blue Atlantic, the living’s easy.Enjoy sailing, snorkeling, strolling pearly pink-sand beaches, and swimming through crystalline turquoise seas on a Bahamas cruise

Mexico

Whether your Mexico cruise style is west-coast Riviera with its Pacific charm, east-coast Caribbean and its island mojo... or perhaps Baja and its out-of-this-world coastal scenery, you’ll find Mexico central to a great vacation.

Australia

Whether you’re after a quick getaway, or a longer holiday you’ll remember forever, an Australian cruise holiday with amazing shore excursions is a great way to relax and take some time out. Excursions fit every lifestyle whether snorkelling the world’s largest reef system, relaxint on Airlie Beach, or indulging in the foodie tastes of Tasmania.

Europe

Few places in the world pack in as much culture per square meter as Europe does. There’s so much variety across the continent and its islands, you have options for experiencing many of these places by sea. Western Europe cruises visit the region from which the explorers of the New World kicked off their voyages.

Alaska

If you’re looking for a cruise vacation that’s a little bit out of the way and a little bit out of the ordinary, make an Alaska cruise your first choice. Alaska is a natural wonder through and through, and the coastal panhandle region is perhaps the most stunning. Cruising Alaska will also give you a first-row ticket to the best of nature — including fjords, wildlife and mountains galore.
